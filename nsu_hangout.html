📖 How to Play
🎯 Win Condition
🏆
4–6 players: First to lock 3 projects wins. 7–10 players: First to lock 2 projects wins.
♾️
Endless Semester Rule: When the deck drops below 3 cards it reshuffles from the discard pile. No deck-empty loss!
🔄 Your Turn (3 Steps)
1
Draw 2 cards from the deck.
2
Play at least 1 card (up to 2). You cannot pass.
3
Discard down to 5 cards max.
🗂 Your Desk
✅
Build: Play a Good card on your own desk (+1). 3 Good cards = Project Locked!
🔒
Lock: Scored project — permanently safe. Desk clears for your next project.
❌
Sabotage: Play a Bad card on an opponent's desk to remove 1 Good card.
🌪 Wild Cards
🛡️
Miracle Rescheduling Out-of-turn shield! When attacked, click "Block It" to cancel the attack. Or use on your turn to recover 1 Good card.
💣

🌐
